Chapter One
It started
with one
perfect bite.

Lost in Burg didn't come from a business plan or a focus group. It came from a craving — the kind that hits you at 1 AM when every other place has pulled down its shutters.

We found our spot right on Kozhikode Beach, Kerala's most storied stretch of sand, and we set up a grill. The idea was simple: serve the best damn burger on the Malabar Coast, every single night, until 3 in the morning.

No frills. No fluff. Just fire, meat, and people who showed up hungry and left with a story.

The Food
Premium cuts.
Zero
compromise.

Every item on our menu is built around one principle: if it doesn't make you close your eyes on the first bite, it doesn't make the cut.

Our burgers use double-ground beef patties — seasoned simply, cooked hot. Our steaks are prime cuts sourced with the same obsession you'd expect from a proper steakhouse, not a beach shack.

And then there are the Naked Oysters — fresh, briny, served raw with pickled shallots and green apple. Because some nights call for something primal.

What Drives Us
Our three rules.
01

Quality Over Everything

Every ingredient is chosen deliberately. Every cook time is obsessed over. You can taste the difference between rushed and reverent — we choose reverent, every time.

02

The Night is Ours

We open when the sun goes down. We close when the last plate goes out. Late nights have their own energy — we built this place to match it.

03

Beach, Always

Kozhikode Beach isn't our backdrop — it's our identity. The sound of the waves, the salt in the air, the crowd that only the coast attracts. We wouldn't want it any other way.
